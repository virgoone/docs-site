# Privacy Policy

Lark ORG ("we") takes your personal privacy very seriously. To this end, we have developed this Privacy Policy, which explains how we collect, use, share and protect your personal information.


## Information collection


When you visit our website, we collect your Internet Protocol Address, browser information, operating system version, access time and other information.
When you register an account on our website, we also collect necessary information such as your username, email address, password, etc.
We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous statistical information to improve the website.


## Information use


We use the information we collect:


Provide and improve our services
Send you technical messages and updates
Analyze usage data to optimize website experience and functionality
Submitted to third parties for statistical analysis purposes (but does not contain personally identifiable information).
Cookie


We use cookies to store your login information and personalise your settings to enhance your user experience. You can modify your browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may affect some functionality.


## Data Security


We take reasonable security measures to protect your personal information and only essential staff can access it. We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties.


If you have any questions, please feel free to communicate with us through our contact information. We attach great importance to providing you with guarantees about privacy protection.
